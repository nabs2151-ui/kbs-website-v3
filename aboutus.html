Our Story

Khokhar Bartan Store — Two Decades of Trust

Khokhar Bartan Store (KBS) was founded in 2003 with a simple idea: every home deserves quality kitchenware at a fair price. What began as a small family-run shop has, over the past 20+ years, grown into a name that families and households across the region have come to rely on for their everyday and special-occasion needs.
